About Me

Hello! My name is Logan VonGunden, and I am a vehicle enthusiast who loves cars and motorcycles. I have owned several vehicles, each with its own story and experience. This site is my way of documenting my vehicle history and sharing it with fellow enthusiasts.

Fun Facts About Me

  • My first vehicle was a 2003 Toyota Corolla S.
  • I currently own a 2012 Volkswagen GTI, 2023 Yamaha YZF-R7, and a 2007 Suzuki DRZ400S.
  • I enjoy attending group rides with a local motorcycle group, as well as just cruising around town on my bikes and in my car.
